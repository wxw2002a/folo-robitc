Inside + Outside Chores

Future Backlog

Full Idea Stack (No Deletions, Structured by Revision)

Rule: Add notes. Do not delete ideas. Grammar edits are allowed. Add ideas first, curate later.

Safety + Security

  • Save humans from fire with heat-resistant build and thermal sensing.
  • Fight robber with own strength while protecting humans.
  • Use non-lethal defense options and threat classification AI.

Maintenance + Chores

  • Analyze ladder from photo/vision, then climb to change light bulbs.
  • Clean gutters and handle rooftop-edge maintenance carefully.
  • Do both inside and outside chores with adjustable human preference.

Behavior + Atmosphere

  • Read human emotion and display emotion on face/chest screens.
  • Be funny and socially adaptive in family settings.
  • Allow "human atmosphere" mode where tasks are done less intrusively.

Rest + Transform

  • Sleep mode: ball up in corner when idle.
  • Battery rule: auto-go to charger and plug itself in.
  • Chair/couch transform: hands as armrests, head as headrest, cushion mode.

Sensing + Interaction Hardware

  • Eyes are cameras, ears are microphones, voice via speakers.
  • Voice stack can integrate Alexa or Apple HomePod channels.
  • Skin should be touch-capacitive; palm is primary tactile input surface.
  • Hands detect wood, metal, and other material textures.

Bio-Inspired + Environment

  • Skin mimics plant-like healing behavior.
  • Explore indoor oxygen support simulation and air-quality response.
  • Use banana-leaf-inspired flexibility as biomimicry placeholder.

Charging + Energy

  • Robot hand can charge phone by holding.
  • Investigate close-range radio-wave charging capability.
  • Maintain rechargeable architecture with automatic docking logic.

Program Management

  • Refine each function into explicit design requirements.
  • Prioritize prototype features vs. future-phase features.
  • Run materials feasibility and user-experience alignment research.
  • Keep modular architecture for future technology upgrades.
